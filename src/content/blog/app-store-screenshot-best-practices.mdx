# App Store Screenshot Best Practices for 2024

Your App Store screenshots are often the first thing potential users see when they discover your app. In fact, **70% of users make download decisions based on the first impression** - and screenshots are a huge part of that.

## Why Screenshots Matter for ASO

App Store Optimization (ASO) is not just about keywords. Visual assets play a crucial role in converting impressions to downloads. Here's why:

- Screenshots appear in search results
- They tell your app's story visually
- They highlight key features and benefits
- Localized screenshots increase conversion by up to 30%

## Best Practices

### 1. Start with Your Strongest Feature

Your first screenshot should showcase your app's most compelling feature. This is what users see in search results before they even tap on your app listing.

### 2. Use Clear, Benefit-Focused Headlines

Instead of describing features, focus on benefits. Compare these:

- **Bad:** "Photo Editor with Filters"
- **Good:** "Create Stunning Photos in Seconds"

Want to see how [LocalizeShots generates compelling headlines](/features) automatically with AI? Our tool analyzes your app and creates benefit-driven copy that converts.

### 3. Show the App in Action

Use realistic iPhone mockups to show your actual app interface. Users want to see what they'll get before downloading.

### 4. Localize for Each Market

Different markets have different preferences:

- **Japan:** Detailed information, smaller text is acceptable
- **US:** Clean, minimal design with large text
- **Germany:** Emphasis on privacy and data security

### 5. Maintain Visual Consistency

Use a consistent color scheme, typography, and style across all screenshots. This creates a professional, polished appearance.

## Screenshot Dimensions

For iPhone 6.7" display (required):
- **Size:** 1290 x 2796 pixels
- **Format:** PNG or JPEG
- **Quantity:** Up to 10 screenshots
