## Why Use a Dedicated Screenshot Tool

Before comparing tools, let's address why Photoshop or Figma alone isn't ideal for app screenshots:

### Time Efficiency
Professional screenshot tools can reduce creation time from hours to minutes. What once took a designer an entire day can now be accomplished in under 30 minutes.

### Localization at Scale
If you're targeting multiple markets, you need to create screenshots in many languages. Manual translation and recreation is extremely time-consuming and error-prone.

### Consistency
Dedicated tools ensure your screenshots maintain consistent styling, mockup positioning, and branding across all variants.

### Device Mockups
High-quality, up-to-date device frames are essential. Screenshot tools provide the latest iPhone, iPad, and Android mockups without you needing to source them.
